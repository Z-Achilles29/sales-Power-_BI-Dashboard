# sales-Power-_BI-Dashboard
Shopify Sales & Customer Funnel Analysis Dashboard
Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of Shopify sales performance, customer behavior, retention metrics, and regional sales trends. The dashboard transforms raw e-commerce transaction data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and customer funnel analysis. It enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

Business Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends.
Understand customer purchasing behavior and retention patterns.
Identify top-performing products, regions, and payment methods.
Track repeat customer rate and customer lifetime value.
Support strategic decision-making through interactive reporting.

Sales Performance Analysis
Track overall revenue and sales trends over time.

Regional Sales Analysis
Visualize sales performance across states and cities.
Identify high-performing regions and markets.
Payment Gateway Analysis
Compare revenue contribution from different payment methods.
Understand customer payment preferences.
Product Performance Analysis
Identify top-selling product categories.
Compare product-wise revenue contribution.
Interactive Filtering
Dynamic slicers for Gateway, Province, and Measures.
Drill-down analysis for deeper business insights.
